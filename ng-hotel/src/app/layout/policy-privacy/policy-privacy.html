Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21/07/2026

1. Who we are

Lotel ("we", "our") is a hotel booking platform. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website.

2. What data do we collect?

  • Account data: first name, last name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Booking data: stay dates, chosen hotel, number of guests
  • Payment data: processed directly by Stripe. We never store your bank details on our servers.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, cookies

3. Why do we collect this data?

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process your bookings and payments
  • Contact you if needed regarding your booking
  • Improve our service

4. Legal basis for processing

The processing of your data is based on:

  • Performance of a contract (processing your booking)
  • Your consent (account creation)
  • Our legitimate interest (service improvement, security)

5. How long do we keep your data?

Your account data is kept for as long as your account remains active. You may request its deletion at any time (see section 7).

6. Who has access to your data?

  • Our team, strictly for booking management purposes
  • Stripe, for payment processing
  • MongoDB Atlas, our database host

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@lotel.com

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies required for its operation (login session) and, where applicable, third-party cookies from our partners (Stripe).

9. Security

We implement technical measures (password encryption, HTTPS connections) to protect your data against unauthorized access.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any material change will be notified to you.

11. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, contact us at: contact@lotel.com
